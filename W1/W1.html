Top 5 World Cup 2022 Jerseys


1. Uruguay



This is a simple polo design with vibrant colours and no unnecessary elements. Minimalism at its best.
It captured Uruguanian national colours in a fresh new perspective


2. Germany



The approach to include a wide strip on the center ended really well. Metallic gold details gives an outstanding view.
This kit really stands out among other favourites.


3. Brazil



The classic yellow design was improved by adding jaguar-giraffe pattern to the jersey.
Green cuffs and collar with thin blue strip are serves as a fine addition to complete national colours palette.
Another unique feature of this kit is representation of Brazilian flag as a collar button.


4. Japan



Japan made a really stylish kit with integration of national visiting card.
The pattern on jersey is actually a reference to manga pages - famous Japanese comics genre.


5. France



The kit lack of details, but remained gold elements made a really good combination with dark blue to form a royal look.
Suitable for current holders of World Cup
